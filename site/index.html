What it does

Days Left is a minimal macOS menu bar utility. It shows exactly how many days remain in the year, month, and week — always visible, never intrusive.

Year countdown

See days remaining in the current year at a glance. The number updates automatically at midnight.

Month & week

Click the menu bar icon to reveal month and week breakdown alongside the year count.

Launch at login

Enable in Preferences and Days Left starts automatically when you log in. Set it and forget it.

Sleep aware

Wakes up correctly after sleep. No stale numbers, no manual refresh needed.

Native macOS

Built with SwiftUI. No Electron, no web views, no bloat. A real Mac app that feels like part of the system.

Zero distractions

No notifications. No sounds. No Dock icon. Just a number in your menu bar that quietly does its job.

FAQ

How is this different from Day Progress?

Day Progress shows how much of the current day has elapsed. Days Left shows how many days remain in the year, plus month and week breakdown. Different time scale, different mental model.

Does it work with Bartender or other menu bar managers?

Yes. Days Left uses the standard macOS menu bar API and works with Bartender, Ice, Hidden Bar, and any other menu bar organizer.

How does it handle leap years?

It uses the system calendar automatically. In a leap year like 2024, December 31 shows 1 day remaining. In a normal year, it shows 0.

Does it use the network?

No network access at all. Days Left reads your system clock and calculates locally. No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry.

Can I show percentage instead of days?

Not currently. The app is intentionally minimal — days remaining only. If there is enough demand, a percentage option may be added.

Is it native or Electron?

100% native. Built with SwiftUI, compiled for Apple Silicon and Intel. It launches instantly and uses negligible CPU and memory.

How do I uninstall?

Quit the app (click the menu bar icon and select “Quit”), then drag the app from Applications to Trash. That is it. No leftover files.
