# Phase 2 — Gap Analysis

## Gap 1 — Beautiful Year-Native Mac Menu Bar App (The Core Product)
**What's missing:** Progress Bar OSX was #1 on Product Hunt in 2018 (617 upvotes) and has never been meaningfully updated. No modern design replacement exists.

**Sources:**
- https://www.producthunt.com/products/progress-bar-osx (617 upvotes, #1 of day)
- https://www.producthunt.com/products/day-progress (Sindre's day-only app, 2024)
- https://macmenubar.com/year-progress-app/

**WINNING Score:** Pain 9 / Timing 9 → FILE (Core product)

---

## Gap 2 — "Days Remaining" Framing vs. Elapsed %
**What's missing:** Every existing app shows elapsed %. No app shows remaining days as primary. Loss aversion makes "47 days left" hit 2× harder than "87% done."

**Source:** Phase 1 psychology research (Kahneman loss aversion, Dai & Milkman Fresh Start Effect)

**WINNING Score:** Pain 8 / Timing 8 → FILE (Core differentiator, v1)

---

## Gap 3 — Year + Month + Week Stacked View
**What's missing:** No app shows cascading urgency across all three time scales. Week-level drives today's decisions. Year-level is the emotional anchor.

**Source:** Phase 1 competitor landscape (verified no app does this)

**WINNING Score:** Pain 7 / Timing 8 → FILE (v1)

---

## Gap 4 — Quarter Awareness for Professionals
**What's missing:** Quarter Progress launched June 2024 with 1 upvote — distribution failure, not demand failure. PMs, engineers, founders plan by quarter. No menu bar app surfaces Q1/Q2/Q3/Q4 alongside year progress.

**Sources:**
- https://www.producthunt.com/products/quarter-progress-macos-menu-bar-app (1 upvote, June 2024)

**WINNING Score:** Pain 6 / Timing 7 → Consider for v1 (low effort addition to dropdown)

---

## Gap 5 — Shareable Year-End "Wrapped" Moment
**What's missing:** Spotify Wrapped drives massive virality. No menu bar app generates a shareable year-end card. Daily for Mac tried but expired Jan 11 and required 24h of logged time.

**Sources:**
- https://newsroom.spotify.com/2024-12-04/wrapped-user-experience-2024/
- https://dailytimetracking.com/blog/year-in-review
- https://techcrunch.com/2025/12/06/spotify-wrapped-2024-is-almost-here-its-time-to-explore-all-the-copycats/

**WINNING Score:** Pain 7 / Timing 9 → v2 (high virality potential)

---

## Gap 6 — Progress Bar + Goals Linking
**What's missing:** App Store reviewers explicitly request goal tracking tied to the year clock. No app connects the year progress to what you planned to accomplish that year.

**Sources:**
- https://apps.apple.com/us/app/resolutions-2026-new-year/id1601071354 (reviews request goal numbers)
- https://setapp.com/app-reviews/goal-tracker-apps

**WINNING Score:** Pain 8 / Timing 7 → v2 (powerful but scope-expanding)

---

## Gap 7 — Blogger Mindshare (Distribution Gap)
**What's missing:** Major Mac productivity roundups never include a time-awareness app. First quality app in this category to get written up owns the SEO position.

**Sources:**
- https://www.xda-developers.com/best-productivity-apps-mac/
- https://zapier.com/blog/best-productivity-apps-mac/

**WINNING Score:** Timing 10 → Launch strategy, not a feature
